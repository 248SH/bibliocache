    Welcome to Biblio-Cache!

    A browser-based book library powered by the OpenLibrary API, helping you build your personal book collection with ease.

    Biblio-Cache saves your book collection to your browser's localStorage. No data is sent to any server, and your collection is only accessible on this device and browser. Use the Save, Load, and Clear Session buttons to manage your data.
